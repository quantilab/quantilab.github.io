    Welcome!

    Quantlab is a new network group aiming to bring together social scientists at the University of Melbourne and beyond to share insights in quantitative methods and research.

    Co-founders: Seraphine F. Maerz (Political Science) and Irma Mooi-Reci (Sociology).

    We are an inclusive network and welcome everyone interested in learning (more) about new quantitative methods and computational techniques. We believe in knowledge sharing and offer learning seminars as well as online tutorials about innovations in data management and analysis, and a platform for project-related code sharing (R, Stata, Python, R Markdown, LaTeX, Quarto).

    Come and join us for our bi-weekly QuantLunches, RSVP here!
